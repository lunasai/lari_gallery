Through illustration, design, and photography, Larissa explores the relationship of the female body with environment, dreams, memory, and ancestry.

Larissa Sans is a Brazilian visual artist and multidisciplinary designer based in Amsterdam. Graduated in Visual Arts (FPA, São Paulo) and Graphic Design (EBAC), she moves between painting, linoprint, illustration, and brand design — bringing the same depth of intention to a personal commission and to a visual identity.
